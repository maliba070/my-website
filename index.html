why spray plastering?

Less waste and mess

Spray plaster comes premixed. This allows you to store any excess material and can be used again. Material doesn't required water and mixing.

Speed

Spray plaster is few times quicker then traditional plaster.

Smooth finish

Spray plaster gives you smooth, the highest quality finish.

Dries white

Spray plaster dries why, so you don't need prime/mistcoat if you want paint it white.Money saving.
